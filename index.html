Story

Yang Kai starts off as a Trial Disciple of High Heaven Pavilion, where he is unable to advance on the path of Martial Dao due to a birth defect. This all changed when he one day stumbled upon a mysterious Black Book. Later, a Golden Skeleton, impressed by Yang Kai's mental fortitude and attitude, comes from the book and integrates itself into his body.[1] This fixes his meridians and replaces his own skeleton to allow him to finally practice Martial Arts. Yang Kai then quickly becomes adept at cultivation, and blooms as one of the best upcoming talents of High Heaven Pavilion. One day, a Mysterious Small World opens up near High Heaven Pavilion, leading to its disciples entering it with the hopes of possibly receiving the inheritance left behind by the ancient cultivators who fell there. From this event, Yang Kai and Su Yan, an extremely talented Core Disciple of High Heaven Pavilion, receive a Dragon and Phoenix-related inheritance respectively. They also learn and willingly perform the Yin-Yang Joyous Unification Art, which greatly enhances their strength in addition to establishing them as lovers. Later, Yang Kai also manages to acquire the Heaven-defying Soul Warming Lotus during another fortuitous adventure.

After traveling down a branch of the Endless River for some time, Yang Kai eventually finds himself having been ejected outside of the Universe Furnace alongside several immature and lifeless Universe Worlds and Celestial Phenomena. From this experience, Yang Kai deduces that the Universe Furnace, which lies within the void of boundless Chaos, and its Endless River are actually responsible for the creation of the 3,000 Worlds and Ink Battlefield.[265] Now needing to make his way back to the 3,000 Worlds from Chaos, Yang Kai, who is accompanied and assisted by the souls of Shadow Leopard and Fang Tian Ci, embarks on this journey for more than 100 years as the newly bolstered Human Race forces in the 3,000 Worlds manage to recapture some Big Territories from the Ink Clan in his absence. During this time, Shadow Leopard proposes that the 3,000 Worlds is likely just one of many such worlds that the Universe Furnace has created, and that these worlds are simply separated by vast expanses of the Chaos, causing them to be unaware of one another Though at first skeptical, a pensive Yang Kai then very quickly finds this theory to be quite plausible.
